Welcome to the 2024 Ledger Live Happy Buy Day Promotion! The 2024 Ledger Live HappyBuy Day Promotion runs once a month for one year, for 24 (twenty four) hours each time, on the dates communicated by Ledger on its social media accounts (https://twitter.com/Ledger) 1. What is the 2024 Ledger Live Happy Buy Day Promotion? Ledger Live users can buy crypto (Bitcoin (BTC), Ethereum (ETH), Solana (SOL) and many others) through Ledger Live without paying additional fees to Ledger and the Participating Live Apps 2. What fees are waived during the 2024 Ledger Live Happy BuyDay Promotion? The fees that are waived are processing fees applied by Live Apps and Ledger for their services when you purchase crypto through Ledger Live. However, network fees or gas fees (for ETH or ERC20 tokens) paid to network validators may apply. If that is the case, the applicable fees will be stated in the quote submitted before you complete your transaction. 3. Participating Live Apps The Live Apps (services) available on Ledger Live participating in the 2024 Ledger Live Happy Buy Day Promotion include the following apps: Banxa, BTC Direct (up to EUR 500,000 transactions volume), Coinify, LoopiPay, MoonPay, PayPal, Ramp, Sardine, Transak This list is subject to change. The apps participating to Happy Buy Day are recognizable by a "Happy Buy Day" tag on Ledger Live.
