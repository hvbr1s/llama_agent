This article outlines the available colors for the Ledger Nano S Plus, the Ledger Nano X, and the Ledger Stax devices. Ledger Nano S Plus: Available in Matte Black, Pastel Green, Amethyst Purple, Retro Gaming, and BTC Orange. Ledger Nano X: Colors include Onyx Black, Pastel Green, Amethyst Purple, Retro Gaming, and BTC Orange. Ledger Stax: This device is available only in grey. Please note that color availability is subject to stock levels; some colors may be temporarily out of stock. For more information, visit https://shop.ledger.com/pages/hardware-wallets-comparison
